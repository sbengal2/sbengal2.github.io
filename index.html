About

Hello! I am Shashank.

I am a graduate student at the State University of New York at Binghamton. I am currently pursuing a Master's Degree in Computer Science. I shall be graduating in the Spring of 2021. I am actively seeking opportunities in the field of software development.

I was born and raised in the city of Bengaluru, the Silicon Valley of India. I completed my Bachelor's Degree in Electrical Engineering from Sir M Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology. My love for programming brought me to the United States.

While I love coding, my first love will always be football a.k.a soccer in the United States. I also enjoy cooking and driving.

Projects

  1. Impact of Racism on US elections

    A sentimental analysis on how racism has affected the recently concluded US Elections 2020. We scrapped around 40 million tweets from Twitter and 5 million comments from Reddit . Using the data, we try to find out how well it corroborates with the election results

  2. Billboard Chart Search

    A web-application that can be used to search more than 300k songs from the Billboard charts. The application also provides a visualisation of change in music preference over the years

  3. Popularity State Machine for YouTube Channels

    Based on the State Pattern, the system uses the popularity score (calculated by an algorithm using number of likes, dislikes and views of each video present in channel) of an YouTube channel to switch between 4 popularity states.

  4. Microprocessor Pipeline Simulator

    A simulator built using the C language, simulates an out-of-order microprocessor pipeline. Capable of handling more than 200 instructions at once, the simulator takes a sequence of assembly instructions and shows cycle-by-cycle execution
